How old am I today?

To find out how old you are today, enter your date of birth and compare it to today’s date. Your exact age is shown in years, months and days (plus total days and weeks).

How to calculate your exact age

  1. Select your date of birth.
  2. Today’s date is filled in automatically.
  3. Click “Calculate” to see your exact age breakdown.

For age on another date (past or future), use the Age Calculator.

FAQ

Is “How old am I” accurate?

Yes. We use calendar math and include leap years.

Do you store my birth date?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Can I calculate my age for a future date?

Yes — use the Age Calculator page.
