How to calculate age in weeks

  • Select your birth date and an “as of” date.
  • We calculate total days between dates.
  • Weeks = total days ÷ 7 (plus remaining days).

FAQ

Is this age in weeks calculator accurate?

Yes. It uses real calendar dates and includes leap years.

Can I calculate weeks for a future date?

Yes — set the “as of date” to any future date.

Do you store my dates?

No. Calculations run locally in your browser.
