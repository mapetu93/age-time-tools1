How Long Would It Take to Travel X Light Years?

Enter a distance in light years and choose a travel speed to see the travel time in years, days, and hours.

Space Travel Time Calculator
Works with either km/h or a percentage of the speed of light.
Enter a distance and speed to calculate travel time.

How it works

A light year is a distance unit: the distance light travels in one Julian year. We convert light years to kilometers, then divide by your chosen speed to estimate travel time.

Note: This is a simplified estimate and ignores acceleration, deceleration, and relativity effects near light speed.

Examples

Proxima Centauri is about 4.2 light years away. Try 4.2 ly and 10% of c to see an approximate travel time.
