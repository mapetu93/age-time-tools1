How this age calculator works

  • You choose your birth date and an “as of” date.
  • We compute the exact difference using calendar units.
  • You get a clear breakdown: years, months, days + totals.

FAQ

Is this age calculator accurate?

Yes. It uses real calendar dates and accounts for leap years.

Can I calculate my age on a future date?

Yes — set the “as of date” to any future date.

Why do I get a different result than another website?

Some sites approximate months as 30 days. This tool uses calendar months.

Do you store my dates?

No. Calculations happen locally in your browser.
