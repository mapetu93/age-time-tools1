How old will you be in 2050, 2060, or any year?

Simply enter your date of birth and select any year from the quick buttons or type a custom year. The calculator instantly shows your age on your birthday in that year — in years, months and days.

How it works

  1. Enter your date of birth.
  2. Click a popular year (2030, 2040, 2050…) or type any custom year.
  3. See your exact age on your birthday in that year.

Why use this calculator?

Whether you're curious about how old you'll be at a major milestone year like 2050 or 2060, planning for retirement, or just wondering about the future — this tool gives you an instant, accurate answer for any year you choose.
