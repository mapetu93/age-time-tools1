How old will you be in 2050, 2060, or any year?

Enter your date of birth and select any year from the quick buttons or type a custom year. The calculator instantly shows your age on your birthday in that year — in years, months and days.

How the calculation works

We take your birth date and build the same month and day in the target year, then compute the exact calendar difference. If you were born on February 29 and the target year is not a leap year, we use February 28.
