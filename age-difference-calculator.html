FAQ

Is this age difference calculator accurate?

Yes. It uses real calendar math and includes leap years.

Does it matter which date I enter first?

No. The tool shows the absolute difference and tells you who is older.

Do you store my dates?

No. Everything is calculated locally in your browser.
